Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up as a helper?

To sign up as a helper, create an account on our platform and select the "I want to help others" option during registration. You'll then be prompted to complete your helper profile, including your skills, availability, and service areas.

How does payment work?

Our platform facilitates connections between helpers and those who need assistance. Payment arrangements are made directly between the parties involved. Some helpers offer their services for free, while others charge a fee. This information is clearly displayed on helper profiles.

Is there a verification process for helpers?

Yes, all helpers go through a verification process that includes identity verification and, depending on the services offered, may include background checks. Additionally, our review system allows users to rate and review helpers based on their experiences.

What if I have a problem with a service?

If you encounter any issues with a service, we encourage you to first communicate directly with the helper. If the issue cannot be resolved, you can submit a complaint through our platform, and our support team will assist in resolving the matter.

Can I offer multiple types of services?

Absolutely! Helpers can offer multiple services based on their skills and expertise. Simply add all relevant services to your helper profile, along with details about your experience and qualifications for each service type.
